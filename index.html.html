The Journey

Trace our strictly monitored logistics chain keeping nature’s perfection intact from the Andes to Singapore's Cold Storage supermarkets.

Step 01 — Origin

High-Altitude Hand-Picking

Sourced from premium plots in Ancash & La Libertad valleys at altitudes up to 2,500 meters, ensuring slow ripening and high concentration of health-preventive antioxidants.

Step 02 — Processing

Zero-Sugar Gentle Dehydration

Water is extracted at controlled low temperatures without artificial preservatives. The natural juices concentrate organically inside each berry, maintaining the essential Nutri-Grade A status.

Step 03 — Packaging

Modified Atmosphere & Kraft Doypack

Packed under a Modified Atmosphere (MAP) to prevent oxidation. We use biodegradable kraft paper pouches to adhere strictly to global green targets.

Step 04 — Logistics

Reefer Container Cold Chain (12°C - 15°C)

Shipped securely across the ocean inside modern, temperature-controlled reefer containers. Consistent humidity and a tight 12°C to 15°C range prevent nutrient loss.

Step 05 — Retail

Singapore Delivery & Cold Storage

Direct distribution to high-end Cold Storage locations, organic stores, and CBD office pantries across Singapore, delivered fresh and ready for busy urbanites.
